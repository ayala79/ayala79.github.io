Buddhist Meditation

Since 2009, Buddhist meditation became the most important aspect in my life.

What is Buddhism?

Buddhism includes philosopy and practice that can bring a lot of benefit to quality of our lives, to our health, heart and brain. Through Buddhist practice,which includes different kinds of meditations and studies, I learned to connect to myself, accept myself and to develop better qualities of mind, such as patience, love, compassion, clear seeing, mindfullness and more.

Many aspects of my life have improved through this practice, including relationships, purpose in life, general wellbeing, health, sleep, relaxation, and happiness.
