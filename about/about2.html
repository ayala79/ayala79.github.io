About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Dechen, I'm 41 years old. I live in Tel-Aviv, which is Israel's almost-capital city. A year and a half ago I came back from some years abroad, in which were dedicated to Buddhist practice and service. Coming back to Israel, I had the fortune to start working as retreats coordinator in the Insight Meditation Society center.

Why Web Design?

The web is the medium of almost all aspects in our lives. From studying, to shopping, to news updates, entertainment, and more. In all fields of interests in my life, the web is one of the key features. I would like to help organizations assemble their websites correctly, so it is easy to navigate and find things. I see many websites that are not designed correctly and I feel as though it is such a shame that their potential is not fully shown. It is as if there is a store which has many precious items, yet they are hidden by clatter and the customers cannot reach or even glimpse at these items. In this case, both the customer and the owner are loosing.
